About me

Hi, I'm Zyldjan Lanticse, a Computer Science student with too much free time and too curious for his own good.

My Passions

Backend Development

I love the problem-solving that comes with playing around with backend technologies; demonstrating how data is stored, transferred, and interpreted. One of my go-to backend frameworks is Python Django, as it strikes a balance between development speed and design consistency.

Cybersecurity

My passion for cybersecurity stems from the fact that bits of data, represented as 1's and 0's, can change unexpectedly and without notice. This inherent unpredictability drives me to develop software solutions that address and mitigate these security challenges. You will begin to notice once you see my projects.

Operating Systems

It all started when a specific feature in Windows became the last straw that broke the camel's back. This experience prompted me to explore other operating systems, and in hindsight, it turned out to be a blessing in disguise. It allowed me to appreciate a variety of operating systems, particularly the Linux derivatives.

DIY Electronics

Since I was little, I was always fascinated of bringing computers to 'life', to have some sort of tangible function -- a thing you couldn't feel with software alone. Doing that now, I realized it wasn't all too hard. Whenever, I get burnt out of coding, tinkering with electronics became my shelter and comfort.
